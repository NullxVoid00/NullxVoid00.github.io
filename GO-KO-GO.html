📖 The Dark Tale

The GO! GO! Grand Prix

When Ko, a demon girl kart racer, enters the world-renowned "GO! GO! Grand Prix," she's seeking more than just victory. With her mentor Gi recently deceased, she hopes the mysterious grand prize can bring him back to life.

But this competition harbors dark secrets. Under the eccentric host Fuelle Von Wheel's twisted entertainment, racers begin disappearing mid-race. What starts as mysterious vanishings soon escalates to brutal murders, leaving contestants terrified and confused.

As Ko investigates the disappearances, she uncovers a web of supernatural horror, past betrayals, and deadly wishes. The killer seems to take special interest in her investigation, making every race a potential death trap.

The Mystery Deepens

With each race, the stakes grow higher. Bodies appear, alliances form and shatter, and Ko must balance her detective work with staying alive on the track. The other racers each harbor their own secrets and motivations, making everyone a potential suspect... or victim.

As the field narrows and the killer grows bolder, Ko faces an impossible choice: pursue her personal goal of reviving her mentor, or save the lives of her fellow racers. In the end, true victory may require the ultimate sacrifice.

🎮 Racing Meets Mystery

🏁 Dynamic Racing

Core Racing: Mario Kart-style gameplay with power-ups, shortcuts, and obstacles.

Narrative Events: Racers disappear mid-race, tracks change dynamically, killer chase sequences.

Investigation Integration: Bump rivals for clues, find evidence pickups, explore secret investigation areas.

🔍 Detective Work

Visual Novel Segments: Explore locations, interrogate suspects, review evidence between races.

Clue Gathering: Racing performance unlocks new investigation options and story progression.

Character Relationships: Build trust and gather information from fellow racers.

⚡ Escalating Stakes

Early Game: Optional bonus objectives for gathering clues during races.

Mid Game: Investigation becomes integrated with racing success.

Late Game: Forensic objectives become critical mission requirements for survival.

🏆 Multiple Modes

Story Campaign: Experience the full murder mystery racing adventure.

Free Racing: Traditional kart racing without narrative pressure.

Multiplayer: Race with friends using unlocked characters and tracks, plus combat racing modes.
