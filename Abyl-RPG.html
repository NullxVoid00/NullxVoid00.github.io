COMBAT MECHANICS

Combat That Keeps You Thinking:

Forget mindless button mashing! Every turn is a tactical decision that could mean victory or defeat. Do you go all-out attack? Play it safe with defend? Use that precious MP for a game-changing spell? The choice is yours!

  • Phase-Based Strategy - Players act together, then enemies retaliate. Coordinate with your party to focus fire on dangerous foes or spread damage strategically!
  • Speed Matters - Faster characters act first in each phase. Build a speedy Rogue to always get the jump on enemies, or accept the Warrior's slower speed for their incredible durability!
  • Risk vs Reward - Defending cuts damage in half but costs your turn. Time it right to survive devastating boss attacks!
  • Critical Hits - That satisfying moment when you land a critical strike! Boost your Luck stat to see those beautiful damage multipliers more often!
  • MP Management - Powerful abilities drain your MP. Spam them early and you'll be stuck with basic attacks when you need them most!

Every Action Has Its Target:

  • Single Enemy - Pick your victim! Perfect for focusing down the biggest threat or finishing off weakened foes!
  • All Enemies - Why choose? Unleash devastating AoE attacks that hit everything! Watch the entire enemy party's HP bars drop!
  • Single Ally - Be the hero your party needs! Target specific allies for healing, buffs, or revival!

  • Glowing Indicators - Valid targets light up with clear visual markers. No guessing who you can hit!

  • Audio Feedback - Satisfying sounds confirm your selection. You'll KNOW when you've locked onto your target!
  • Smart Auto-Targeting - For AoE and self-targeted abilities, the system handles targeting automatically. Just choose the skill and watch it work!
