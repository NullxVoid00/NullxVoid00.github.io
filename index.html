EGO/TRIP

Platform: VRChat

Genre: Multiplayer VR Roguelite

Players: 1-4 Co-op

A VRChat roguelite RPG serving as a prelude to Ego/Dive. Control Null and echo fragments through randomized dungeons within the mysterious Red Shard. Features permanent Remnant upgrades, multiple endings based on party composition, and a hub area called Mender's Bar.

EGO/DIVE

Platform: PC (More TBA)

Genre: Psychological Adventure RPG

Players: Single-player

After an incident leaves their host comatose, personality fragments split into the subconscious world of Absentia. As Null, dive through seven distinct layers, choosing to help, ignore, or forcefully absorb the fragments. Features fusion mechanics, strategic grid combat, and three major story routes.

EGO/TRIP

"This space, contained within a crimson crystal called the Red Shard, serves as a sort of waiting room between consciousness and deeper unconsciousness."

Core Gameplay Loop

Start each run at Mender's Bar, select your character and Remnant loadout, then venture into procedurally generated dungeons. Fight enemies, collect items, defeat bosses, and unlock permanent upgrades. Runs increase in length as you progress: 20→40→60→80→100 rooms.

Game Systems

⚔️
Risk/Reward Leveling

Gain XP by defeating enemies, but lose it when hit. Max level 5 per run with enhanced abilities at each level.

💎
Remnant System

Collect emotional remnants for permanent upgrades. Low, Mid, and High grade remnants provide different benefits.

🎯
Quest System

Mender offers 3 optional objectives per run, providing extra rewards and story context.

📦
Inventory Management

Limited slots (4-8 items) during runs. Store up to 30 items permanently at Mender's Bar.

The Labyrinth's Corrupted

The Serpent

A giant snake coiled in the sky. Players walk along its back destroying weak points while avoiding spikes and flying enemies. The sky swirls red with intense music.

The Queen

A headless Victorian woman with rotating arms as platforms. Her decapitated head flies around attacking while players scale the hands to reach weak points.

The Charade

A suited entity with 3 floating masks. Break them in order: Sadness→Anger→Happiness. Each mask changes the battlefield differently.

The Moon

Controls an endless ocean below while shifting phases. Eventually lowers to player level, holding itself with water hands for attack opportunities.

The Self (N_)

A white hooded figure with a Hulking Shadow. Mimics fragment abilities while the Shadow assists with projectiles and swipes.

Mender (Secret Boss)

The bartender himself. Quick, agile, with area attacks and the ability to separate players for one-on-one combat.

Multiple Endings

Your ending depends on party composition and notes collected:

  • Husk Ending: Null Solo - Return to repeat the labyrinth indefinitely
  • Obsession Ending: Null + L_ - Stay in pursuit of meaning never found
  • Narcissist Ending: Null + M_ - Remain to collect riches forever
  • Coward Ending: Null + D_ - Freeze in terror, never leave again
  • Missing Ending: All fragments - Attempt breakthrough for eternity
  • True Ending: All fragments + All notes - Shatter the Red Shard and begin the true journey

EGO/DIVE

"The main fragments of their personality split off into a subconscious world named Absentia."

The World of Absentia

A layered space where fragments exist after traumatic splitting. Shaped like an egg with segments stacking top to bottom. Each fragment has a domain reflecting their personality and inner state. The environment can be altered by fragments with sufficient willpower.

The Seven Layers:

  1. Null's Layer: Bleak minimalist realm with black, white, and gray. Weak creatures populate the empty landscape.
  2. Dolor's Layer: Geometric fortress prison with calming sanctuary interior. Fragile creatures and dangerous Mares outside.
  3. Midas' Layer (Ambrosia): Bustling green-hued city of eternal night. Insomnia tower casts shadows over hedonistic pleasures.
  4. Limerence's Layer: Pitch black with a solitary theater. Backstage reflects isolation while shadowy audiences fill the seats.
  5. Neuro's Layer: Graveyard raining discarded memories from crimson skies. Bodies constantly arise as Neuro tries to keep them buried.
  6. Subconscious Layer: Wild abstract architecture with no ruling fragment. Hostile and ever-changing.
  7. The Lighthouse: Purple ocean with red sunset sky. The lighthouse floats alone, its interior changing based on your journey.

Core Mechanics

🎭
Fusion System

Two fragments combine into new beings with blended traits. Each fusion has unique personality, abilities, and appearance.

🌀
Creation Powers

Fragments can manifest objects and alter reality through willpower. Limited by imagination and exhaustion.

⚡
Grid Combat

3x3 grid battles with position-based strategy. Front row deals/takes more damage, abilities vary by position.

🔄
Reset System

Time loops occur under certain conditions. Most inhabitants lose memories, environments reconstruct.

💧
Drowning Events

Extreme despair causes tears to flood spaces. All beings dissolve and reform, triggering resets.

🌈
The Veil

Appears during destruction route. Grows with each absorbed fragment, showing accumulated powers.

Key Concepts

Mares & Demi-Mares

Creatures created through negative emotions or corruption. Mares are deformed and vicious, while Demi-Mares retain some control and enhanced abilities despite mental fracturing.

Red Shards & Pools

Connection points between spaces serving as checkpoints/teleports. Can capture moments in time for saving. Shards shatter into pools when activated.

Cicero's Elevator

Non-euclidean space moving throughout Absentia. Reach extends with more fragments present, appearing in impossible locations.

Fragment Weapons

  • Null: Knuckle knives held backwards for slashing
  • Dolor: Force field ripples as boxing gloves/shields
  • Midas: Baseball bat that doubles as a shotgun
  • Limerence: Scythe-halberd hybrid with heart design
  • Neuro: Serrated trench shovel for killing and burying
  • Cicero: Book containing knowledge and imaginary concepts

Combat System

Strategic turn-based battles on a 6x3 grid:

  • Back 3x3 for party, front 3x3 for enemies
  • Position affects damage dealt/received
  • Timed button presses for attack effectiveness
  • Paper Mario-style guard system
  • Adjacent allies can perform combo moves
  • ATB meter enables Fusion moves when full
  • Environmental objects provide tactical options

  • Forlorn (Dolor + Limerence): Paralyzed by self-doubt and dark fantasies
  • Heyday (Limerence + Midas): Unrestrained enthusiasm for life's pleasures
  • Manic (Neuro + Limerence): Wild mood swings and shocking extremes
  • Usurper (Neuro + Midas): Ruthless ambition with charismatic facade
  • Marionette (Neuro + Dolor): Lethargic puppet controlling others with invisible strings

THE WORLD & ITS MYSTERIES

The Fracturing

When trauma shatters a mind beyond repair, the pieces don't simply disappear. In the world of Ego/Dive, they manifest as living beings within Absentia - a layered subconscious realm where each fragment builds their own domain, develops their own will, and struggles with their purpose.

Key Concepts

Fragments

Main personality aspects given form. Each represents different emotions and traits, with their own abilities, weapons, and domains in Absentia.

Figments

Beings created by Fragments. Have varying autonomy and contain remnants of their creators' emotions. Can become Mares if corrupted.

Remnants

Emotional fragments that modify abilities. Come in Low, Mid, and High grades. Both a story element and gameplay mechanic.

The Fragments' Stories

Dolor - The Fortress of Tears

Awakening alone and afraid, Dolor's uncontrolled abilities nearly drowned him in his own tears before creating Bolster for protection. He built a geometric fortress but chose to protect rather than strengthen his fragile creatures. His forms shift between sadness (blue), rage (red Ire), and rare joy (yellow Voluptus).

Midas - The Fallen King

Resentful of restrictions, Midas built the city of Ambrosia devoted to hedonistic dreams. When unchecked vice corrupted his citizens, he donned a disguise and abandoned his tower Insomnia. Now he fights as a masked vigilante to save his people from Flourish, the Demi-Mare who seized control.

Limerence - The Eternal Performance

Once close to Idalia, Limerence's obsession became all-consuming. Given the virtual world Aldora to find self-love, he instead descended into endless battles with copies of himself, dying countless times. His theatrical nature masks deep loneliness and destructive patterns.

Cicero - The Knowledge Keeper

Manifesting with Null's memories in his book, Cicero repeatedly tried to unite the fragments only to face endless resets. His elevator serves as sanctuary and transport between layers. Despite failures eroding his memories, he remains determined to guide Null differently each cycle.

Three Paths to Resolution

🌟
Self-Acceptance

Help and understand each fragment. Face their traumas, build connections, and work toward true healing. The path of empathy and growth.

⏸️
Repeat Pain

Avoid confrontation and maintain distance. Neither heal nor destroy, leaving all unchanged. The path of stagnation.

💀
Self-Destruction

Forcefully absorb fragments through violence. The Veil grows with each kill, granting power at the cost of self.

The Lighthouse Mystery

At Absentia's deepest point lies the Lighthouse - a structure whose interior transforms based on your journey. What awaits within depends entirely on the path you've walked and the choices you've made.

Will you unite the fragments and face the truth together?
Or will you forge ahead alone, whatever the cost?
