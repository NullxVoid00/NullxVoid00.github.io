Welcome to the Battlefield

Dive into an epic PVP shooter set in iconic levels from the classic game, including Bob-Omb Battlefield, Whomp's Fortress, and Tick Tock Clock. Battle it out with custom Mario-themed guns across eight faithfully recreated arenas, plus a secret level to discover. Choose your character and engage in multiple game modes. Explore a vibrant hangout area, enjoy remixed and classic music, and use the YouTube search feature to set the perfect vibe. With tons of customization options, there's always something fun to do. Choose Your Faction

🍄 Team Mario
The Mushroom Kingdom's finest. Led by the iconic plumber himself, Team Mario embodies heroism and versatility. Known for their acrobatic skills and resourcefulness, they excel in adapting to any battlefield situation.
🐢 Team Koopa
Bowser's elite force. Team Koopa combines brute strength with cunning strategies. Their tough shells provide natural defense, while their fire-breathing abilities make them formidable in both close combat and ranged warfare.
💰 Team Wario
Treasure hunters and mischief-makers. Team Wario specializes in unorthodox tactics and explosive weaponry. Their greed drives them to control resource-rich areas, often using their signature dash attacks to quickly traverse the battlefield.
👻 Team Shyguy
A collective of masked warriors renowned for their loyalty and tenacity. Team Shyguy's strength lies in their unity and determination. Despite their small stature, they use coordinated tactics to overcome any challenge.
🎩 Team Black Hat
A shadowy faction shrouded in mystery. Team Black Hat's true motives and allegiances remain unknown. They employ unpredictable strategies and seem to appear and vanish at will, leaving both allies and enemies questioning their role.

Arsenal of Destruction

🌱 Piranha Plant Pellet Gun
Ammo: 30
Damage: 35
A Standard Assault Rifle Type, this gun shoots seeds at high velocity from its water containment tank and is a reliable choice for beginners and experts alike. Features a diagetic ammo counter.
🎯 Piranha Plant Pellet Sniper
Ammo: 1
Damage: 200-700
This sniper rifle, modeled after the Red Piranha Plant, is a precision weapon designed for expert marksmen. With proximity-based damage, built-in adjustable scope, and devastating long-range potential.
⚡ Spark Pistol
Ammo: 6
Damage: 70
The Spark Pistol fires bouncy fireball bullets that ricochet off walls, making it perfect for catching opponents around corners or creating chaos in enclosed spaces.
💣 Bob-Omb Launcher
Ammo: 3
Damage: 100
Inspired by the explosive Bob-Omb, this grenade launcher packs a punch. Its mega blaster design hurls explosive rounds that deal heavy damage on impact, perfect for taking out clusters of enemies.
🚀 Bill Blammer
Ammo: 1
Damage: 200
A compact rocket launcher that packs a punch, the Bill Blammer fires a single powerful Bullet Bill with precision. Enhanced splash damage makes every shot count.
💧 L.L.U.D
Ammo: 100
Damage: 15
Based on the iconic F.L.U.D.D. from Mario Sunshine, this short-ranged flamethrower spews a continuous stream of scorching water, ideal for close-quarters combat with infinite ammo.
🔨 POW Hammer
Ammo: ∞
Damage: 70
A classic giant hammer that not only delivers solid melee damage but also grants a speed boost while holding the trigger. Swing this mighty weapon to clear out enemies up close.
🎨 Wonder Brush
Ammo: ∞
Effects: Various
This magical brush can summon various objects into existence. Perfect for support players. Desktop users can use 'B' to Paint and 'C' to Cycle abilities.

Wonder Brush Abilities

💧 Water Bubble
A slippery water bubble that disorients opponents and slows their movement, making it harder for them to aim or escape.
🛡️ Shell Shield
A sturdy, large shell that serves as a temporary shield, absorbing incoming damage or doubling as a platform for tactical positioning.
🎨 Paint Ball
A quick, low-damage projectile of paint that can be thrown in a pinch to chip away at an opponent's health or distract them.
🌪️ Tornado
A swift-moving tornado that barrels forward, bouncing opponents into the air and disrupting their flow.
🏃 Trampoline
A handy bounce pad that propels players upward, providing a quick escape or allowing access to higher ground.
⚙️ Rotating Platform
A rotating platform that aids in traversal, offering strategic movement options in complex environments.
🔥 Flame Turret
A flame-throwing turret that defends a designated area with intense heat, deterring enemies from entering for a brief period.
🍄 Mushroom
A small health pickup that restores a bit of health, giving you just enough of a boost to stay in the fight. Now pickupable!

Halloween & Version 1.5 Update

🆕 New Leveling System and Unlockables

  • Implemented a brand new Experience and Leveling System
  • Gain EXP for time spent in the world and for kills
  • Unlock special perks at certain level milestones
  • Added early implementation of unlockables (currently one available)
  • Added groundwork for Code-Based Save and Load system for saving between instances

⚔️ Weapon and Combat Enhancements

  • Significantly improved sniper mechanics with enhanced aim accuracy
  • Introduced proximity-based damage: more damage at greater distances
  • Added functional and adjustable scopes for precision targeting
  • Increased reload time on sniper rifle for better balance
  • Added Diagetic Ammo Counter to Piranha Plant Pellet Gun
  • Increased splash damage of Bill Blammer
  • Made WonderBrush usable for desktop users, using "B" to Paint and "C" to Cycle

🎃 Halloween Special

  • Introduced an Optional Halloween Theme
  • Added spooky jump scares throughout the world
  • Implemented additional Halloween decorations
  • Added a festive pumpkin hat for players to enjoy

🎮 New Gameplay Features

  • Improved hat tracking to players' heads with option to lock hats to head
  • Added falling walkway to Big Boo's Haunt
  • Implemented more accurate health and shield display for Mario 64
  • Added Kill notifications to HUD
  • Allowed Mushrooms to be picked up
  • Added darkness slider to Peach's Castle
  • Introduced additional avatar selections for optional avatars

🔧 Quality of Life Improvements

  • Balanced Character Stats and abilities
  • Optimized gun meshes and materials
  • Overall increased performance across the game
  • Fixed the long-standing gun jamming issue
  • Resolved collision issues on grated surfaces
  • Corrected various sound issues
  • Fixed Mini map trigger in Peach's castle
  • Made guns drop upon teleport to fix "Missing Guns" glitch
