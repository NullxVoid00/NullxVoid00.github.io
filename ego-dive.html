EGO/DIVE

Journey Through the Fractured Psyche

When trauma shatters the mind, the pieces don't disappear. They take form, build worlds, and struggle with purpose. As Null, the hollow core of what once was whole, you must navigate Absentia and decide the fate of your scattered self.

WHAT IS EGO/DIVE?

A Psychological Journey

After an incident leaves their host in a comatose state, the main fragments of their personality split off into a subconscious world named "Absentia." Each Fragment has built their own layer, developed their own personality, and struggles with their own demons. As Null, you must dive through seven distinct layers, choosing to help and understand the fragments, ignore their plights and remain a husk, or forcefully absorb them - with each path leading to vastly different outcomes.

THE WORLD OF ABSENTIA

A layered space where fragments exist after traumatic splitting, shaped like an egg with segments stacking from top to bottom.

Layer 1: Null's Domain

A bleak, minimalist realm painted in black, white, and shades of gray. Weak, harmless creatures populate this empty landscape where Null first awakens. The starting point of all journeys, representative of the hollow state left behind after fracturing.

Layer 2: Dolor's Prison Fortress

Geometric shapes float around an imposing prison exterior, remnants of Dolor's power experiments. Inside, a calming sanctuary contradicts the fortress appearance. Fragile creatures wander outside while dangerous Mares lurk in shadows. Dolor hides within, protected by the loyal knight Bolster.

Layer 3: Midas' Ambrosia

A bustling, green-hued city of eternal night where hedonistic pleasures mask darker truths. The towering Insomnia club casts both literal and figurative shadows. Once Midas' paradise, now corrupted and controlled by the Demi-Mare pop idol Flourish.

Layer 4: Limerence's Theater

Pitch black realm with a solitary theater providing the only light. Traditional stage filled with shadowy audience silhouettes. Backstage reflects isolation while the virtual forest Aldora hides deeper horrors - endless battles with dark copies of Limerence himself.

Layer 5: Neuro's Graveyard

A bleak domain where discarded memories rain from crimson skies as static bodies. Neuro constantly tries to bury the rising dead, representing Null's rejected traumas. Unlike others, Neuro remembers between resets, using this knowledge to test visitors harshly.

Layer 6: Subconscious Chaos

Wild, abstract architecture with no ruling fragment. A hostile environment where the rules of reality bend and break. The only layer without a direct creator, representing the untamed depths of the psyche. Navigation requires all fragments working together.

Layer 7: The Lighthouse

A purple ocean beneath red sunset skies. The lighthouse floats alone on a small island, always active. Its interior transforms based on your journey - the final destination where all paths converge and ultimate truths await.

Supporting Cast

VOID

The most loyal companion in all of Absentia, Void manifested as a Figment - an embodiment of the host's beloved pet and their sole source of true vulnerable connection. Unlike the Fragments, Void maintains an almost dog-like demeanor, reflecting his origins.

Armed with a knife he carries in his mouth, Void provides crucial emotional support and moral grounding for Null's journey. His unwavering compassion and innocence represent the host's lost capacity for trust and connection - qualities essential for becoming whole.

As a Figment rather than Fragment, Void uniquely cannot be corrupted by Neuro's base abilities, though he remains susceptible to becoming a Mare. His inquisitive nature often helps uncover things others would miss.

IDALIA

A powerful Figment and guardian of Absentia, Idalia appears as a red-haired, porcelain-skinned figure with mannequin-like joints and no facial features. She alone controls the resets of Absentia and retains memories between loops - a burden that has cost her sanity over countless cycles.

Once living near Absentia's center with Limerence, she made the painful choice to leave him, understanding that staying would perpetuate their illusory existence. She gave him Aldora as both a gift of self-discovery and a replacement for their shared fantasy.

Now aligned with Neuro in his harsh methods, Idalia believes that sometimes the cruelest truths are necessary for growth. Despite her lost faith in the fragments' ability to save themselves, she remains highly empathetic and intelligent, serving as arbiter of this fractured world.

BOLSTER

A bird-like knight in blue armor, Bolster is Dolor's most devoted protector and closest friend. Created in Dolor's moment of desperate need when his tears threatened to drown him, Bolster embodies unwavering loyalty despite his quirky, clumsy nature.

Taking himself extremely seriously, Bolster vows to protect not just Dolor but everyone in their layer from outsiders. A prominent scar marks his face - a reminder of when Dolor's Ire form struck him in an overwhelmed state. This incident led Dolor to lock Bolster out of the prison for his own safety.

Bolster views Dolor as both a parental figure and a child needing protection. Despite his knightly duties, he secretly wishes he could join Skim and Parlor's podcast crew, revealing a more playful side beneath his serious exterior.

FLOURISH

A Demi-Mare with green skin and pink hair, Flourish wears a white horned theater mask - now half-cracked after her confrontation with Midas. Once a poor street child in Ambrosia, she witnessed her mother's descent into Mare corruption during the city's fall.

Forced to kill her own transformed mother in self-defense, this trauma triggered Flourish's partial Mare transformation. Retaining her sanity but gaining immense power, she leveraged her new abilities to dominate the entertainment industry as a hypnotic pop idol.

Now controlling Insomnia tower through mind-controlled victims and strategic cunning, Flourish harbors deep resentment toward Midas for neglecting the city's vulnerable during his hedonistic reign. Her motivations blend justified anger, past trauma, and the corruption of her Demi-Mare nature.

SKIM & PARLOR

The dynamic podcasting duo of Ambrosia, these super fans turned journalists chronicle their city's stories from their trusty van. Skim appears as a green germ-like fellow wearing a red hoodie that covers his lower face and a barret, while Parlor is a short purple cat with red eyes and a green hoodie who serves as the show's main host.

Both idolize Midas and have followed his career since his glory days. Now they unknowingly track the masked vigilante cleaning up the streets - never realizing it's their fallen idol himself. Their genuine love for their city and its people drives their reporting.

While they can seem naive to some, their dedication to uncovering truth and spreading information makes them valuable allies. They view the fragments as Midas' extended family, referring to them as his "brothers" in their coverage.

MIMI

A young girl appearing only as a black silhouette, Mimi clutches a worn teddy bear given to her by Limerence. As one of the few truly aware occupants of Limerence's theater, she remains trapped within its walls, more conscious than her fellow audience members.

Once a devoted fan of both Limerence and Idalia's performances, Mimi was devastated when their shows suddenly stopped. Now she waits in lonely confusion, unable to understand why Limerence abandoned the theater or her place in this strange world.

Her interactions with others reveal a child seeking connection and understanding. She views Limerence as a father figure and star, supporting him despite not comprehending his actions. Her presence serves as a reminder of those affected by the fragments' choices.

YOUR JOURNEY AWAITS

Three paths lie before you. Each leads to a different truth.

Path of Self-Acceptance

Help and understand each fragment. Face their traumas together, build genuine connections, and work toward true healing. This path requires empathy, patience, and the courage to confront painful truths. Unite the fragments and awaken with growth and self-love.

Final Boss: Shiva - A floating distorted figure with multiple phases

Path of Repeat Pain

Avoid confrontation and maintain distance. Neither heal nor destroy, leaving all unchanged. The easiest path in the moment, but one that ensures the cycle continues. Reunite fundamentally unchanged, doomed to repeat old patterns.

Final Boss: Vishnu - Multi-armed figure wreathed in black mist

Path of Self-Destruction

Forcefully absorb fragments through violence. The Veil grows with each kill, granting immense power at the cost of self. Each absorbed fragment adds their color to your flowing cape. Destroy any chance at reunification.

Final Boss: Brahma - A rainbow heart that dims as you fight

Major Encounters

Bolster - The Devoted Knight

The False Idalia

The Amalgamation

A towering fusion of Limerence's dark copies, attacking while he confronts the False Idalia. Null and companions must hold it off during the emotional climax.

The Truth of the Fracturing

The Host's Story

A young boy, battered by an abusive upbringing, did his best to protect his younger sister from their father's drunken rages. On a rain-drenched night, he arrived home to find his father striking his sister. Lunging to intervene, the boy was cast aside as his sister fled into the downpour.

Chasing after her desperately onto the slick streets, his vision blurred by rain and tears. The headlights came too fast. The impact shattered his psyche entirely as he fell into a coma, mind adrift in Absentia.

The Fragments' Origins

Dolor's Creation

Awakening alone and afraid, Dolor's uncontrolled abilities nearly drowned him in his own tears. In desperation, he subconsciously created Bolster to save himself. His fortress represents the boy's desire for safety from abuse, while his fragile creatures embody vulnerability he couldn't show.

Midas' Fall

Resentful of Null for restricting their expression in real life, Midas built Ambrosia as ultimate freedom. When unchecked hedonism corrupted his citizens into violence, he felt the weight of responsibility. His vigilante persona represents the boy's desire to fix mistakes and protect others.

Limerence's Obsession

His destructive love for Idalia mirrors the boy's desperate need for affection never received at home. Aldora's endless deaths represent self-punishment for perceived inadequacy. The dance with False Idalia is accepting that some love must be let go to grow.

Neuro's Rejection

Originally vulnerable and concerned, Neuro was rejected as Null tried to bury trauma. Awakening in the graveyard of discarded memories, surrounded by attacking corpses of past pain. His appearance distorted by rage, he killed Null repeatedly before Idalia granted him memory between resets. Now he tests others, believing harsh truth creates growth.

The Lighthouse Truth

The Lighthouse interior transforms based on your journey:

  • Self-Acceptance: A warm, healing space where fragments unite willingly. The host awakens changed but whole, ready to face life with support.
  • Repeat Pain: A cold, sterile hospital room. The host awakens unchanged, mentally detached, doomed to repeat cycles.
  • Self-Destruction: A shattered mirror realm. Killing the host's representation potentially ends their actual life.
